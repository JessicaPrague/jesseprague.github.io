Daily UI Challenge

To flex my creativity and improve my design skills, I took on the Daily UI Challenge

During the summer of August 2017, I challenged myself to design a different screen every day in order to sharpen my visual design skills. DailyUI sent me a daily User Interface design prompt, and I gave myself 60 minutes to brainstorm an idea/problem, ideate, and create a final design on Sketch. This exercise helped me get creative and grow as an overall designer! Pictured below are a few of the designs I created.
